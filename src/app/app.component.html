CHRISTIAN DIOR

The Dior Book Tote, part of the Dioramour capsule, is an original creation introduced by Maria Grazia Chiuri, Dior's Creative Director, and has become a staple of the Dior aesthetic. The design is part of the Dioramour capsule and is fully embroidered with the red and white D-Royaume d'Amour motif, imagined by Pietro Ruffo and further adorned with the 'CHRISTIAN DIOR' signature on the front. The small tote exemplifies the House's signature savoir-faire and may be carried by hand or worn over the shoulder.
